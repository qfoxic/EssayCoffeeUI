Progressive Delivery

Progressive Delivery is an outstanding service launched by our company to manage large or complex orders, and to keep clients updated about the progress of work in hand.

The advantages are:

  1. You can have your academic paper delivered gradually, as our writer progresses;
  2. You do not have to pay the whole amount for your thesis or dissertation at once. Your payment is broken down into several affordable installments;
  3. You have a personal manager responsible for your order, who constantly informs you of the progress of your assignment;
  4. We provide you with a personal schedule that saves your time, and lets you know exactly when your work will be delivered;
  5. We revise your paper free of charge within 21 days after the first order’s approval.

This method of managing orders was created after a thorough examination of our customers’ needs and the academic writing market in general. Since fraudulent practice has been found within the industry, we want you to feel secure with us.

Progressive Delivery gives you a chance to:

  • minimize the risk of getting a low quality paper;
  • avoid major revisions, because you receive it stage by stage;
  • avoid investing money in a paper you cannot observe being completed.

How much does it cost and how can you order it?

Our Progressive Delivery service costs only 10% more than the original price of your paper. To include this feature, simply check the "Progressive Delivery" box in your order form and get an instant quote.

Limitations:

Please note there are some limitations to this feature - it is not applicable on orders with deadlines from 8 hours to 3 days and/or a total value of less than $200, but is mandatory for papers with a deadline that exceeds or equals 7-10 days and a total price over $600.
