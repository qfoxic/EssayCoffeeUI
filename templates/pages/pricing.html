About the Pricing Policy

Prices at EssayCoffee.com are set to the industry average, allowing us to employ writers who meet the high standard demands applied to your work, using the US and European writers who have professional experience developing the best quality research for all your coursework, even your written dissertations and thesis work.

We provide assistance in a wide range of assignments, from personal letters to book/music reviews, from ordinary research papers to Master’s Degree theses. Some clients simply ask us to revise the papers they finished themselves - to meet specific grammar or formatting standards, while others give us detailed outlines to ensure the paper meets a clearly defined set of standards and requirements. These details are important to your success, your success is about meeting higher standards, accomplishing better work, and overcoming the challenges of school.

Your free inquiry provides you with an estimated price and deadline for your work, including a writer «on-hold» for you - it’s better than putting a book on-hold at the library, because you know your writer will be there for you when pay for your assignment. You’re a marketing student - we look for a marketing professional. You want a nursing paper, we use a nurse who writes for our company. Our writers are professionals in their industry and their quotes and availability are designed to meet your specific needs.

If you provide all the necessary data and articulate your order properly, your writer will quickly be able to assess and complete your order in a timely manner. A clear order is more likely to receive a paper that fits all requirements. This is key to providing that exceptional quality that makes our organization a top choice for students looking for professional services.

We’re very flexible, and discuss each assignment on a personal basis; above all, we value communication. If you help us, we’ll help you, and the benefit will of course be mutual.
