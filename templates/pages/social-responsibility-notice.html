Dear customers and visitors to our website.

As a business, we are not only responsible to our customers and writers to fulfill our main business functions, but must also adhere to established general social morals and tenets.

THIS POLICY DOES NOT APPLY TO SUBJECTS OTHER THAN MEDICINE and similar (as listed above).
